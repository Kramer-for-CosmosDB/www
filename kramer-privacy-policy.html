Privacy Policy

Kramer does not directly collect or store personal information. As a Windows Store App, Kramer may be subject to data collection by Microsoft Error Reporting or App Analytics. Such data may be provided to the Kramer development team, but is considered strictly confidential, is never sold or shared with third-parties, and is used strictly for improving Kramer.

As an internet client submitting user input (e.g. SQL queries) to a remote server, Kramer does not attempt to filter or restrict the user from entering personal information. Users are responsible for data entered into and transmitted via Kramer. Kramer makes no gaurantees about the service located at the user-configured endpoint.
